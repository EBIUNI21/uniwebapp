With PetPals, we aimed to provide users and pet owners with a safe space to ask questions, no matter how trivial, and to receive expert advice from fellow users and veterinarians. On this website, users can showcase photos of their pets and engage with other animal enthusiasts. They can upload images of any potential health issues their pets might be experiencing, allowing them to gain the necessary support. Additionally, users can search for terms that may help them find solutions to specific problems they may encounter. This interaction enables users to engage with the website without feeling exposed. Furthermore, users can utilise this platform to promote and display their products, making it the ideal space for animal lovers and pet owners.
